Georgia State University (GSU)

Location: Atlanta, Georgia, USA

Quick Facts

  • Founded: 1913
  • Type: Public research university
  • Enrollment: ~52,000 students
  • 12 colleges including Robinson College of Business & Andrew Young School
  • Ranked #1 public university for undergraduate teaching (multiple years)

Programs

Over 250 degree programs across 100+ fields including business, arts, sciences, law, and health.

Tuition & Costs

  • In-state: ~$12,500/year
  • Out-of-state: ~$33,320/year

Admissions

Requires transcripts, GPA, SAT/ACT (varies by year), essays, and proof of finances for international students.

Pros & Cons

  • Pros: Diverse student body, urban setting, strong academic reputation.
  • Cons: Large size may mean less personal attention, downtown Atlanta living costs.
